Network Analysis Tool

"Complex systems are systems whose behaviour is intrinsically difficult to model due to the dependencies, competitions, relationships, or other interactions between their parts or between a given system and its environment." Biology is packed with complex systems which are often challenging to comprehend. Genetta represents genetic designs as networks. This representation allows analysis and transformations to be applied to tailor visualisation based on requirements.
